FAQ

Common Questions.

We inspect for shingle age, granule loss, flashing failures, decking damage, and interior leaks. Most roofs over 15 years old showing multiple issues are better candidates for replacement than repeated repairs. We'll tell you the truth either way — we don't push replacements when a repair is the right call.
Most policies cover wind and hail damage. We work directly with insurance adjusters and help you navigate the claims process from inspection through final payout. Most clients end up paying only their deductible — or less.
Most residential replacements are completed in one to two days. We handle permitting, tear-off, installation, and full cleanup. Larger homes or complex rooflines may take an additional day.
Every installation is backed by our lifetime workmanship warranty plus the manufacturer's material warranty — typically 30 to 50 years depending on the shingle line. If it fails due to our work, we fix it at no charge.
